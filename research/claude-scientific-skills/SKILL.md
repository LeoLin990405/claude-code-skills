- **Visualization**: matplotlib, plotly, seaborn, scientific-visualization
- **ML/AI**: scikit-learn, PyTorch Lightning, transformers, DeepChem

- **Documents**: docx, pptx, pdf, xlsx, LaTeX posters
- **Statistics**: statsmodels, statistical-analysis, pymc
